Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

EduCycle is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user of our campus marketplace.

{% for section in sections %}{% empty %}

1 Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, student ID, department, year of study, and phone number.

Listing Data: Item names, descriptions, prices, photos, and category information you provide when creating listings.

Transaction Data: Order history, payment method preferences (we do not store card numbers), and delivery addresses.

Communication Data: Messages exchanged between buyers and sellers through our platform.

Usage Data: Pages visited, search queries, and interaction patterns to improve our service.

2 How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To facilitate buying and selling transactions between students
  • To send notifications about your listings, orders, and messages
  • To verify student identity and maintain platform safety
  • To improve our platform features and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

3 Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Between Users: Your name and listing details are visible to other students. Your email is only shared if you choose to display it in your profile settings.

Payment Processors: Payment data is handled by Stripe and Razorpay under their respective privacy policies. We never store full card numbers.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the safety of our community.

4 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure password hashing, and regular security audits.

Your password is stored as a cryptographic hash — we cannot see it. If you forget it, you must reset it.

While we take security seriously, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and enable two-factor authentication when available.

5 Your Rights

Access

Request a copy of all data we hold about you

Correction

Update inaccurate personal information via Settings

Deletion

Delete your account and all associated data from Settings

Portability

Request your data in a machine-readable format

6 Cookies

We use essential session cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences (such as theme and notification settings). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings, though this will log you out.

Questions about your privacy?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us.
