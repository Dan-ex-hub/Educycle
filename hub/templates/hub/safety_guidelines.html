Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our top priority. Follow these guidelines for every transaction.

Important: EduCycle facilitates connections between students but is not responsible for individual transactions. Always exercise caution and trust your instincts.

Meeting Safely

Meet in public places

Campus library, cafeteria, student centre, or department building

Meet during daylight hours

Avoid late-night or isolated meetups

Bring a friend

For high-value items, bring someone you trust

Tell someone where you're going

Let a friend or family member know your plans

Verifying Items Before Buying

  • Inspect thoroughly — Check for damage, missing parts, or signs of tampering before paying
  • Test electronics — Power on devices, check all functions work as described
  • Verify edition/version — For textbooks, confirm the edition matches what was listed
  • Ask questions first — Use our messaging system to clarify any doubts before meeting
  • Never pay before seeing — Do not transfer money before physically inspecting the item

Payment Safety

Use cash for in-person meetups

Cash is the safest option for campus transactions

Use our secure payment system

For online payments, use EduCycle's built-in payment options (Stripe/Razorpay)

Never share bank details

Do not share your bank account number, UPI PIN, OTP, or card CVV with anyone

Beware of advance payment scams

Legitimate sellers will not ask for payment before you've seen the item
