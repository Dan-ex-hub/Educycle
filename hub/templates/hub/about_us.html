About EduCycle

Connecting students through sustainable commerce — one textbook at a time.

Our Mission

Built for students, by students

EduCycle was born from a simple observation: students constantly need to buy and sell items, but existing platforms are either too complex, too expensive, or too impersonal.

We wanted to create something that feels like it was built specifically for campus life — sustainable, affordable, and community-driven.

Sustainable Commerce

Every transaction helps reduce waste and promote reuse on campus.
